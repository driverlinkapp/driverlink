The Roadmap: Zero to $90K

1
Get Your CDL Permit
Study the CDL manual, pass the written knowledge test at your DMV. Cost: ~$50-$100 in fees. Takes about 1-2 weeks of studying.
2
CDL Driving School (3-8 weeks)
ELDT-certified training program. Classroom + behind-the-wheel. Most programs are 4 weeks full-time. Cost: $3,000-$7,000. Many carriers offer tuition reimbursement if you drive for them.
Pro tip: Some companies pay for your school entirely if you commit to 1 year
3
Entry-Level Driving (Year 1)
Start with general freight or local delivery to build experience and a clean record. Get your feet wet, learn the industry, earn while you learn.
$45,000 - $55,000/yr
4
Add Tanker + HAZMAT Endorsements (Year 1-2)
Tanker (N) endorsement: written test, no ELDT required. HAZMAT (H): ELDT course (~$49-$69 online) + TSA background check ($85) + DMV fee. Get the X (combo) endorsement to unlock the highest-paying specialty hauling jobs.
Total endorsement cost: $200-$400. Pay bump: +$10K-$20K/yr
5
Specialty Hauling Career (Year 2+)
With 1-2 years experience, Tanker + HAZMAT endorsements, and a clean record, you're now one of the most in-demand drivers in the country. Fuel hauling, chemical transport, propane delivery, HAZMAT tanker. Companies compete for you.
$65,000 - $105,000+/yr
